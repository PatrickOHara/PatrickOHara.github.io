A few words summerising what WMLG does...

My research focuses on graph and optimisation algorithms for NP-hard problems with applications to air pollution. I am part of the Clean Air in London project which aims to develop machine learning algorithms to understand and predict air quality in London. The algorithms I develop take these pollution models and find trajectories which minimise the pollution of exposure of a person following the trajectory.
